An ozone generator is a device that produces ozone, a toxic gas made of three oxygen atoms (O3), as a way to purify air or water: Air purification Ozone generators are marketed as odor removers and air purifiers, but they are ineffective at removing particles, biological contaminants, and chemical pollutants.
